Ceviche

     

TAGS: Peruvian, Seafood
Printer Friendly Version

Ceviche is a traditional Peruvian dish enjoyed worldwide comprised of raw fish slow cooked by lime juice.

   
       

INGREDIENTS

4 - 6 slices tilapia or sea bass (or 2 pounds)
1 cup lime juice (8-9 large green limes)
1 red onion
1/2 Peruvian red pepper
1 clove of garlic
  Salt and Pepper to Taste
4 - 6 Sweet potatoes
1 cob Sweet corn

PROCESS

1. tilapia or sea bass (or 2 pounds)
2. Pour the cup of lime juice into the bowl and mix with fish.
3. Cut slices of onion, red pepper and garlic. Add both to the bowl and mix in with pepper and salt to taste. Let sit as you prepare the sides.
4. Boil sweet potatoes until tender. Peel and cut in halves. In another pot, boil the sweet corn for 15 minutes.
